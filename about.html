What it is, what it isn't

It is a desktop app you install. Your API keys. Your data. Your machine. No subscription. No telemetry. 7-day trial, then an unlock code.

It isn't a SaaS wrapper. We don't proxy your AI calls. We don't collect your conversations. We don't sell data. We never will.

It is free to use with Ollama for local models — zero cloud required if you don't want it.

It isn't finished. Beta. Expect rough edges. Report them.
